About Me

Hailing from the wild and wonderful West Virginia, I'm a Southern gal who enjoys too much pimento cheese and a strong gin drink when the moment is right. I love helping others (social worker at heart and by education), problem-solving, and dreaming up grand plans. After working in politics, fundraising, and community outreach, I'm regenerating (Doctor Who fan FTW) again as a full stack web developer.

When I'm not learning code, you can find me running around RVA with my better-half, Pres, eating, drinking, and laughing 'til it hurts.

If you're interested in working together, please fill out the contact form or connect with me on LinkedIn. I look forward to hearing from you!
